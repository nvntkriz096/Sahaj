About Sahaj Genetics

Specialist genetic care, made calm and human.

Genetic questions often arrive at sensitive moments — a worrying test result, a difficult pregnancy, a family history that won't leave your mind. Our role is to turn that uncertainty into understanding, and understanding into a plan you feel good about.

Led by an experienced medical team, Sahaj Genetics combines accurate diagnostics with non-directive counseling that follows international standards. We explain the science in plain language, respect your choices, and stay with you from the first question to the next step.

What we offer

Diagnostics and counseling, tailored to you.

Genetic Diagnostics

Karyotyping, chromosomal microarray, and next-generation sequencing panels to pinpoint the genetic basis of a condition with accuracy.

Genetic Counseling

Calm, non-directive sessions that help you understand your risk, weigh your options, and make decisions that are right for you.

Prenatal & Preconception

Screening and guidance before and during pregnancy, including support after recurrent pregnancy loss or an abnormal scan.

Pediatric Genetics

Evaluation of developmental delay, dysmorphism, and metabolic or chromosomal conditions in newborns, infants, and children.

05

Cancer & Hereditary Risk

Assessment of familial cancers and late-onset disorders, so you can act early with informed screening and prevention.

06

Carrier & Premarital Screening

For couples planning marriage or pregnancy — including consanguineous relationships — to understand inherited risks together.
